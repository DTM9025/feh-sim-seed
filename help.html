Settings

Goal

Choose a goal from the dropdown menu. The options are:
  • Any 5* - take every orb that has a focus unit in that color, and continue until any focus or non-focus 5* unit appears.
  • Any focus - take every orb that has a focus unit in that color, and continue until any focus 5* unit appears.
  • Red/blue/green/colorless focus - choose one of the focus units in that color. Take every orb of that color and continue until that focus unit appears, ignoring other 5*s and other focus units, even ones sharing the same color.
The count option makes it simulate until the chosen goal has been reached multiple times, for answering questions like "can I get my +4 to +10 on this banner?"

Banner selection

Select the starting rates from the dropdown menu.
Enter the number of focus units that the banner has on each color in the R/B/G/C boxes.

Results

The graph shows how many orbs you need to spend to get a certain percent chance of reaching your goal, with labels at a few milestones for hard numbers. Each label shows the number of orbs spent before the indicated percentage of simulated results reach the goal.
Don't forget that there is no amount of spending that can guarantee that you reach the goal. The 99th percentile shows a really high cost, but one out of every hundred people who read this will spend more than that next time they go to summon.

FAQs

How do split focuses work here?

Let's take the first Legendary Banner as an example. You want Fjorm, but don't care about Xander or Caeda as the other two blue options. If you select blue focus as your goal, then the result will be how much you need to spend to get that Fjorm, no matter how many Xanders or Caedas you get along the way.
In a future update, there will be an advanced goal selection that will let you set more complicated goals like "get either this focus unit or this other one", or "get at least one of every focus unit".
